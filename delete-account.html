MBill — Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: 27 November 2025

If you wish to permanently delete your MBill account and all associated data, you may request deletion using one of the methods listed below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: In-App Request

  • Open the MBill app
  • Go to Profile
  • Select Delete Account

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to contactnareshsankhla@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete my MBill account"

Please include:

  • Your registered email or phone number
  • Confirmation that you want permanent deletion

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your business profile
  • All customers and invoices
  • Saved documents and uploaded images
  • Local backups generated by the app

All associated data will be removed from active systems immediately and permanently deleted from backups within 90 days.

What May Be Retained

For security, fraud prevention, and product improvement, MBill may retain:

  • Anonymous analytics logs (non-identifiable)
  • Diagnostic crash reports

These are kept only for up to 90 days.
