Privacy Policy for MBill – GST Invoice App

Last updated: 27 November 2025

MBill (“the App”), developed by Naresh Kumar Sankhla, is a billing and GST invoicing application that helps users create and manage invoices. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how your information is protected.

1. Information We Collect

The app stores your business, customer, and product data only on your device. We do NOT collect or transmit any personal data to our servers.

We store locally on your device:

  • Business details (name, GSTIN, address)
  • Customer details (name, contact, GSTIN)
  • Invoice data, invoice numbers, items
  • Products and services added by you

No sensitive personal data (Aadhaar, PAN, biometrics, etc.) is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data stored in the app is used only for:

  • Generating GST-compliant invoices
  • Maintaining your invoice history
  • Storing your products, customers, and business profile

We never upload or sell your data. It always stays on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs that collect personal information.

No analytics, ads, or tracking tools are integrated.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally using secure device storage. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, all stored information will be permanently deleted from the device.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is NOT intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store data from children.

6. Permissions Used

WRITE / READ STORAGE: To create and export PDF invoices.

CAMERA (optional): Only if you use invoice scanning features (no data sent to server).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
